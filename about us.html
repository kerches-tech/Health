Our Story

A legacy of
compassionate care

Founded in 1980, Meridian Hospital began as a small community clinic with a single, clear purpose — to provide quality healthcare that is accessible, dignified and compassionate. Over four decades, we have grown into a multi-disciplinary private hospital equipped with state-of-the-art facilities and a team of over 80 specialist doctors.

Our growth has always been guided by the needs of our patients. Every new department, every piece of technology, every specialist we bring on board is in response to the evolving health needs of the communities we serve. We are proud to be a hospital where clinical excellence and human warmth exist side by side.

Today, Meridian Hospital serves over 40,000 patients annually, earning a 99% patient satisfaction rating — a number that reflects not just the quality of our medicine, but the quality of our care.

Our Purpose

What drives
everything we do

Our Mission

To provide every patient with world-class medical care delivered with kindness, respect and clinical precision — regardless of background or circumstance. We exist to heal, to comfort and to improve the quality of life of every person who walks through our doors.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and respected healthcare institution in the region — a centre of medical excellence where innovation meets compassion, where every patient feels safe, heard and cared for, and where our community's health outcomes continue to improve year on year.

Our Promise

We promise every patient timely, transparent and compassionate care. We promise our staff a respectful environment where excellence is recognised. And we promise our community that we will continue to invest in the people, technology and programmes that improve health outcomes for all.

Core Values

The principles that
guide our practice

01

Patient First

Every decision we make — clinical or operational — begins with what is best for the patient. Their comfort, dignity and recovery always come first.

Our Journey

Four decades of
milestones

1980

Founded

Meridian Hospital opened its doors as a small community clinic with 10 beds and a passionate team of 5 doctors committed to quality care.

1990

First Major Expansion

Expanded to a full hospital facility with 80 beds, added Surgical and Maternity departments, and brought in specialist consultants.

2000

ICU & Emergency Unit

Opened a dedicated 24/7 Emergency Unit and Intensive Care Unit, establishing ourselves as the region's most comprehensive emergency care provider.

2008

ISO Accreditation

Achieved ISO 9001 certification and COHSASA accreditation — formal recognition of our commitment to international clinical quality standards.

2015

Digital Diagnostics

Invested in a state-of-the-art 3T MRI, 64-slice CT scanner and full digital pathology lab — transforming our diagnostic capabilities.

2020

Cardiac Centre of Excellence

Launched our Cardiac Centre with a bi-plane catheterisation laboratory, offering primary PCI within 60 minutes around the clock.

Today

Continuing to Grow

With 80+ specialists, 20+ departments and 40,000+ annual patients, we continue to set the benchmark for healthcare excellence in the region.
