Common Questions

Frequently asked
questions

You can book online via the Book Appointment button, call us on 0504209812, email info@meridianmedical.co.uk, or use the contact form above. We'll confirm your appointment within 2 hours.

No referral is needed for most of our specialist consultations. You can book directly. If your GP has issued a referral letter, please bring it along — it can help provide useful background information.

Please bring a valid photo ID, your insurance card or payment method, any relevant medical records or test results, and a list of any medications you are currently taking.

Yes, we accept most major health insurance providers. Please contact us with your insurance details before your appointment so we can confirm coverage and arrange billing directly with your insurer.

Most routine blood tests are available within 24 hours. Imaging results are typically ready within 2 business days. Your doctor will advise you on expected timelines during your consultation.

General visiting hours are 10am – 12pm and 4pm – 7pm daily. ICU and maternity ward visits may have different arrangements. Please call the relevant ward directly or contact reception to confirm before visiting.
