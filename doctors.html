Doctor Spotlight

Dr. Sarah
Okonkwo

MBBS · FRCP · FESC · Interventional Cardiologist

Dr. Okonkwo is one of West Africa's foremost interventional cardiologists, with over two decades of clinical experience at some of the world's leading cardiac centres. She completed her specialist training at the Royal Brompton Hospital, London, and holds dual fellowship with the Royal College of Physicians and the European Society of Cardiology. Under her leadership, Meridian's Cardiac Centre achieved its first primary PCI programme — reducing door-to-balloon times to under 60 minutes.
